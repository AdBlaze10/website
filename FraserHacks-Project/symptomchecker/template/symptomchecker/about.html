About SymptoSense

SymptoSense is a hackathon demo app that lets users select symptoms and view possible matching conditions along with an overall triage level.

What it does

  • Collects symptoms through a form
  • Matches those symptoms against a predefined condition dataset
  • Applies triage rules and red-flag overrides
  • Shows educational next-step advice

What it does not do

  • It does not diagnose medical conditions
  • It does not replace a doctor, nurse, or emergency services
  • It is not a clinically validated medical tool
Disclaimer: This tool is for educational and hackathon demonstration purposes only. If symptoms are severe, worsening, or involve an emergency, seek care from a licensed healthcare professional immediately.
